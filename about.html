About Me

Hi, I'm Leslie Huynh!

I am a senior at NYU Tandon, graduating Spring 2022 with a Bachelor’s of Science in Integrated Design and Media. I am an aspiring brand designer, with a background in business and marketing.

My previous experiences include being a communications intern for the non profit organization, Games for Change, and a marketing intern for New York City based IT company, ETech7.

I have challenged myself to learn Illustrator and InDesign as a part of my volunteer position with ARMY Magazine, a non profit that releases bi monthly magazines about South Korean boy band BTS, as their SNS and Editorial designer.
